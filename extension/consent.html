Connect Web Annotator for Pi to Pi

Sending an annotation shares these details with the Pi process on your computer:

  • the page URL and title;
  • the selected element, text, and nearby source;
  • the note you wrote.

This data first goes to Pi at 127.0.0.1. The extension does not send it to the developer or an analytics service. Pi may then send the annotation content to your configured model provider.

Firefox controls this optional permission. You can remove it later from the extension's Permissions and data settings.
